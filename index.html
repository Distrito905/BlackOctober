Black October

Report of the violence episodes that occurred in Black October (2.017) in Catalonia.

-Before the process:

  • A father and his daughter attacked for carrying the Spanish flag (Nationalists insult the child.)
  • Independentistas attack two women from «Barcelona with the Spanish National Team» (Three skinheads beat up the two girls from the association.)

-September 20. Pave the way:

  • Close to the Civil Guard Command. There are women and children inside (Attack on the Manresa barracks.)
  • Nationalists put up posters on the barracks wall and insult the Guardia Civiles (Attack on the Berga barracks.)

-September 21:

  • They begin to encircle the «Economia» so that guards and court secretaries who have entered by a court order can not leave (They keep them from getting information about the coup d' état.)
  • Assault on two Spanish Police cars at «Economía» (This is how the cars of the Guardia Civil left in the protests of Catalonia.)
  • They jump over the Guardia Civil cars (In the cars there were two rifles that the nationalists stole.)

-October 1:

  • Two cars of the Guardia Civil are stoned (Nationalists throw stones.)
  • Mossos d'Esquadra attacks the Guardia Civil (The Catalan police fail to comply with the judge's mandate.)

-October 2:

  • Police officers attacked in Calella (National Police in Calella.)
  • More attacks on the spanish police in Calella (Calella at the night.)

-October 3. Strike:

  • Catalan Police attack a citizen who disagrees with the strike (Mosso attack a citizen.)
  • Catalan police help the strike (Mossos close a supermarket.)

-After the process:

  • They tried to burn down a house because they had the Spanish flag (Balsareny's attack.)
  • Members of the Arran nationalist party attack the press (Attack on the headquarters of the «Crónica Global».)
